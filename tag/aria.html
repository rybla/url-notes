The n-Category Café
aiai safetysafeguarded aicategory theorysoftware engineeringariatopos instituteformal verificationcatcolabmathematical proofsai risk management
  • Project: "Safeguarded AI" meeting in Bristol.
  • Participants: 50 category theorists and software engineers.
  • Funding: £59 million from ARIA (UK's Advanced Research and Invention Agency).
  • Goal: Develop a "gatekeeper" AI system to manage and reduce risks from other AI agents by combining scientific world models with mathematical proofs.
  • Rationale for Category Theory: To create software that is more flexible and easier to formally verify.
  • Tooling: Topos Institute is demonstrating CatColab, a category-based software for building models.
