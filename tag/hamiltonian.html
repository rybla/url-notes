  • Topic: The origin of the ground state energy term (+1/2) in the quantum harmonic oscillator's Hamiltonian.
  • Question: Can the Duflo isomorphism explain the correction term that distinguishes the quantum Hamiltonian (H = 1/2(p^2 + q^2 + 1)) from the classical one (1/2(p^2 + q^2))?
  • Obstacle:
    • The Hamiltonian H is in the center of the universal enveloping algebra of the Heisenberg algebra.
    • The Heisenberg algebra is nilpotent.
    • The Duflo isomorphism appears to provide no corrections for nilpotent Lie algebras, which contradicts the hypothesis.
