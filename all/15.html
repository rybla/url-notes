published: D:20181208153818-06'00'
  • Thesis: Present existence is evidence for reincarnation, particularly if the universe's history is infinite.
  • Core Argument:
    • If time is infinite (past and future) and individuals live only once, the probability of being alive now is zero.
    • Since you are alive now (a certainty), this implies either the past is finite or individuals can live more than once.
  • Mechanism:
    • Given an infinite future, every qualitative state will recur infinitely.
    • This includes the specific state that created you. A sufficiently precise repetition of these conditions would constitute a new incarnation.
  • Personal Identity:
    • Theories of personal identity that preclude reincarnation also create the zero-probability paradox when combined with an infinite past.
    • Therefore, present existence is evidence against such restrictive theories.
  • Justification for Infinite Past:
    • The concept of a "beginning of time" is metaphysically incoherent, as "coming into existence" presupposes a time before that existence.
    • This is analogous to the impossibility of a physical "edge of space."
