  • Project: Zedless, a privacy-focused, local-first fork of the Zed editor.
  • Core Tenets:
    • Removal of all proprietary, non-self-hostable cloud service dependencies.
    • Elimination of telemetry and automatic crash reporting.
    • Prioritization of user-configurable infrastructure for all network-dependent features, which are disabled by default.
  • Contribution Policy:
    • Does not require a Contributor License Agreement (CLA); contributors retain their copyright.
  • License Compliance:
    • Utilizes cargo-about for automated dependency license verification within the CI pipeline.
    • Configuration for license acceptance and clarification is managed in script/licenses/zed-licenses.toml.
