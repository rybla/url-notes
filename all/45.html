published: 2025-08-11
  • Legal Action: The Wikimedia Foundation lost a judicial review challenging the UK's Online Safety Act regulations.
  • Core Conflict: Wikimedia argued that the act's "Category 1" classification, intended for large social media sites, was too broad and improperly applied to Wikipedia.
  • Primary Concern: If classified as "Category 1," Wikipedia might be forced to implement identity verification for its contributors, which it claims would undermine the privacy and safety of its volunteer editors.
  • Court's Ruling: The High Court dismissed the challenge to the lawfulness of the government's decision-making process for the regulations.
  • Future Implications: The judgment leaves open the possibility for future legal challenges, specifically against Ofcom's potential decision to classify Wikipedia as "Category 1" or if the act's implementation makes the site's operation unviable.
